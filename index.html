What is the school board?

The CPS board runs Chicago's public schools - they set policies, raise taxes, and control the fate of neighborhood schools.


We don't elect it. Why does that matter?

In most districts, residents elect the school board. In Chicago, the Mayor appoints it.

As a result, the board is unaccountable to the residents using neighborhood schools.


What's the elected school board referendum?

On Nov. 6th, you have the chance to change that. In certain precincts, you can vote YES for an elected school board.
